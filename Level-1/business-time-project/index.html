Pumpkin-Oh Treats

Let your dog enjoy these delicious pumpkin and molasses treats, in moderation of course. Pumpkin is a natural source of fiber, great for improving digestion. Fiber is often fermented into fatty acids. This fatty acid then helps to prevent the overgrowth of any bad bacteria and helps the colon recover from injury.

Ingredients: organic canned pumpkin, molasses, water, olive oil, wheat flour, baking soda, baking powder

Peanut Butter-Oh Treats

Let your dog enjoy these delicious peanut butter cookie treats, in moderation; of course. Peanut butter is an excellent source of protein and healthy fats, vitamins B and E, and niacin. Fats and oils in the diet keep your dog's coat shiny and healthy and are also important in reproduction. B vitamins are necessary in a dog's diet for the assimilation of protein and fat. E vitamins defenses against oxidative damage. Niacin treats inflammatory skin conditions in dogs, such as lupoid or pemphigoid conditions.

Ingredients: peanut butter, wheat flour, eggs, water, honey, cornstarch
